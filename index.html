Issue 02 · High
Geographic Targeting
What We Found Not Resolved
Location targeting is set to "United States (country)" with no further segmentation. For the two brands, targeting should reflect how each one operates:

WAFS is a local firm serving Mid-Missouri. Targeting should focus on the Columbia/Mid-Missouri area, ideally with a radius or at minimum state-level targeting. Running nationally wastes budget on areas with no conversion potential.

BTE serves clients across multiple industries nationally, so broader targeting may be appropriate. However, even for BTE, starting with high-conversion states or regions and expanding from there would be a more strategic use of the budget.
Previously detected and raised. This was flagged before. No changes have been made.
Issue 03 · High
Conversion Goals: Account-Default
What We Found Needs Definition
Conversion goals are set to "Account-default," meaning no specific conversion actions have been defined. Google does not know whether to optimize for a phone call, a form submission, or a booking. Defining clear goals would significantly improve how the algorithm allocates budget.
Previously discussed. The plan was to use phone calls and lead generation as goals, but this has not been configured yet.
Issue 04 · Medium
Daily Budget: $7.00/day
The budget is set at $7.00/day (~$210/month). For a Performance Max campaign distributing across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, and Discover, this is below the recommended minimum of $15-20/day. At this level the algorithm does not collect enough data to learn and optimize.

The plan was to start at $250/month and scale to $500 after reviewing early performance. Since the campaign has been paused with zero data, no evaluation has been possible.
Issue 05 · Medium
Sitelinks: Not Configured
No sitelinks have been added. Sitelinks are additional links below the main ad (e.g., "Our Services," "Book a Consultation") that improve ad strength and click-through rate. Google recommends a minimum of 4 for Performance Max.
